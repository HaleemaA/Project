DESSERT!

RECIPIE

WAFFLE

  • 250g of flour
  • Spoonful of sugar
  • Pinch of powdered vanilla
  • Pinch of salt
  • Eight egg yolks
  • Half liter of sweetened cream or fresh milk
  • 250g of butter
  • Eight egg whites

TOPPINGS

Toppings of your choice

    METHOD
  1. 1. In a mixing bowl mix flour, sugar, vanilla, salt, and egg yolks
  2. 2. Blend in half a liter of sweetened cream or fresh milk and then melt the 250 grams of butter in a pan
  3. 3. Whip the eight egg whites until they are very stiff
  4. 4. Add the melted butter to the original mixture followed by the whipped egg whites
  5. 5. Lightly grease a waffle iron and heat
  6. 6. Fill one side with a good spoonful of the waffle mix and close the iron
  7. 7. Cook until it has a golden color
  8. 8. Serve with toppings of your choice
